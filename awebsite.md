## About PuzzleNest

Welcome to PuzzleNest, a modern puzzle platform built for curious minds. Whether you enjoy quick riddles during coffee breaks or deep strategy puzzles that make you think for hours, PuzzleNest gives you a complete experience in one place.

Our mission is simple: make thinking fun, social, and rewarding. We believe puzzles are not just games; they are tools for focus, creativity, memory, and confidence. Every challenge on PuzzleNest is designed to feel engaging, approachable, and satisfying.

From number puzzles to visual logic and word games, each category has multiple levels so beginners can learn comfortably and experts can stay challenged. You can solve at your own pace, track progress, and discover patterns in how you think.

---

## Why Users Love It

### 1) Clean and Friendly Experience
PuzzleNest is designed with clarity first. The interface is minimal, responsive, and easy to navigate on desktop, tablet, and mobile. No clutter, no confusion, just smooth puzzle solving.

### 2) Smart Difficulty Progression
Start with beginner-friendly puzzles and naturally progress to advanced challenges. Our progression system introduces complexity step by step, helping users improve without feeling overwhelmed.

### 3) Daily Challenges
Every day brings a fresh challenge. Daily puzzles are curated to balance fun and difficulty, giving users a reason to return and maintain a healthy puzzle habit.

### 4) Progress Tracking
Keep track of streaks, completion times, accuracy, and puzzle categories solved. Personalized insights help users discover strengths and improve weak areas.

### 5) Community and Competition
Challenge friends, compare scores on leaderboards, and join themed puzzle events. PuzzleNest makes solo play enjoyable and social play exciting.

## Core Features

- **Puzzle Library:** Hundreds of puzzles across logic, math, word, pattern, and lateral thinking categories.  
- **Timed Mode:** Race against the clock to improve speed and focus.  
- **Practice Mode:** Solve without pressure while learning strategies.  
- **Hints System:** Stuck? Get layered hints that guide without spoiling.  
- **Achievements:** Unlock badges for milestones and special wins.  
- **Leaderboard:** Weekly and monthly rankings for top solvers.  
- **User Profiles:** Save preferences, history, and favorite puzzle types.  
- **Accessibility:** Keyboard support, readable typography, and contrast-safe colors.

## Visual Style Guide

- **Primary Color:** Deep Indigo (#3B3F8C) for trust and focus  
- **Accent Color:** Bright Coral (#FF6B6B) for actions and highlights  
- **Background:** Soft Gray (#F7F8FC) for a calm reading surface  
- **Typography:** Clean sans-serif for readability and modern tone  
- **Buttons:** Rounded corners, medium weight text, subtle hover animations  
- **Icons:** Simple line icons with clear metaphors for categories

The visual language should feel smart, playful, and welcoming. Keep motion subtle and purposeful. Prioritize readability and spacing for longer solving sessions.
